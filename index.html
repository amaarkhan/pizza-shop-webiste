About Haj Pizza & Kebab

Located in the historic city of Elvas, Portugal, Haj Pizza & Kebab brings you the authentic flavors of Turkish cuisine. Our commitment to fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and exceptional service makes us the go-to destination for kebabs, pizzas, and Turkish specialties.
